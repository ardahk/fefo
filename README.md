FeFo (Free Food) is a **campus-first** iOS app for discovering and sharing campus events at UC Berkeley. 
Find pizza at seminars, leftover burritos from club meetings, and never miss free food on campus again.

## What it does ğŸ“±

- **Map View** - See all active food events on an interactive campus map with color-coded pins
- **Event Discovery** - Browse events by time, location, or search by building/tags
- **Post Events** - Share free food with the campus community in seconds
- **RSVP System** - Mark Going/Maybe/Not Going to help others gauge availability
- **Comments** - Ask questions or share updates on events
- **Leaderboard** - Earn points for posting events and climb the rankings
- **Profile Stats** - Track your events posted, attended, and community impact
